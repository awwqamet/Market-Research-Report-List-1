<p><p>The Integrated Workplace Management Software (IWMS) market is categorized into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises IWMS involves software installed locally on a company’s servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investments and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based IWMS solutions are hosted on external servers, providing flexibility, easier updates, and lower initial costs, allowing organizations to scale their operations and access data remotely. Each type caters to different organizational needs and operational strategies.</p></p>

<p><p>The mass spectrometer market encompasses various techniques. Accelerator Mass Spectrometry (AMS) allows for the measurement of rare isotopes with high sensitivity. Gas Chromatography-MS combines gas chromatography with mass spectrometry for analyzing volatile compounds, while Liquid Chromatography-MS targets non-volatile compounds. Inductively Coupled Plasma-MS (ICP-MS) excels in elemental analysis. Isotope Ratio Mass Spectrometry (IRMS) focuses on isotope ratios for environmental and biological studies. Ion Mobility Spectrometry-MS separates ions based on their mobility, enhancing analysis. Other methods include various hybrid techniques and evolving applications.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Knockout Cell Lines</li><li>Overexpression Cell Lines</li><li>Inducible Expression Cell Lines</li><li>Knockdown Cell Lines</li><li>Knockin Cell Lines</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell line generation market comprises several types, each serving distinct research needs. Knockout cell lines are engineered to eliminate specific genes, facilitating studies on gene function. Overexpression cell lines artificially enhance gene expression to analyze product effects. Inducible expression cell lines allow controlled gene expression upon stimulation. Knockdown cell lines use techniques like siRNA to reduce gene expression, while knockin cell lines introduce specified genes or mutations. Other markets may include primary cell lines and stem cell-derived lines for diverse applications.</p></p>

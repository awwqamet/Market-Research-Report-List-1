Single Ion Beam Deposition utilizes a single beam for precision layer application, offering control over film thickness and uniformity. In contrast, Dual Ion Beam Deposition employs two beams, enabling enhanced layer properties and improved deposition rates. Both technologies are essential in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, optics, and coatings, providing advanced methods for material engineering and surface treatment.</p></p>

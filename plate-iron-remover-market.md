<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Permanent Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plate Iron-Remover market includes two primary types: Electromagnetic and Permanent Magnet separators. Electromagnetic separators utilize electric currents to generate a magnetic field, effectively attracting and removing ferrous contaminants from materials. They are preferred for applications requiring adjustable magnetic strength. On the other hand, Permanent Magnet separators rely on fixed magnetic fields created by permanent magnets, offering consistency and low maintenance. Both types are essential in industries like recycling, mining, and food processing for enhancing product purity and protecting equipment.</p></p>

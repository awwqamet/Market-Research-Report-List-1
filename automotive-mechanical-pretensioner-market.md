<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Mechanical Pretensioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buckle Pretensioner</li><li>Retractor Pretensioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive mechanical pretensioner market features two primary types: buckle pretensioners and retractor pretensioners. Buckle pretensioners are integrated within the seatbelt buckle, tightening the belt during a crash to reduce occupant movement. In contrast, retractor pretensioners are built into the seatbelt retractor mechanism, retracting the belt to keep the occupant securely in position during a collision. Both types enhance passenger safety by minimizing slack in seatbelts and optimizing restraint effectiveness during impact scenarios.</p></p>

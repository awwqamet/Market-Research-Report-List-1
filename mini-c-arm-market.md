<p><p>The Mini C-Arm market can be categorized into two primary types: Mini C-Arms with Pulsed Fluoroscopy and Mini C-Arms with Continuous Fluoroscopy. Mini C-Arms with Pulsed Fluoroscopy utilize intermittent X-ray exposure to reduce radiation dose while maintaining image quality, making them ideal for procedures requiring precision and safety. In contrast, Mini C-Arms with Continuous Fluoroscopy provide a constant X-ray beam for real-time imaging, offering enhanced visibility during dynamic procedures. Both types cater to varying clinical needs in orthopedic, dental, and surgical applications.</p></p>

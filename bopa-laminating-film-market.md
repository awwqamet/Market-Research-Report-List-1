<p><ul><li>Flat-Film Extrusion</li><li>Tube Film Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>BOPA laminating film, used for packaging and protective applications, is produced through two primary types of extrusion processes: flat-film extrusion and tube film extrusion. Flat-film extrusion creates a continuous sheet of film suitable for applications requiring wide, flat surfaces. In contrast, tube film extrusion forms a cylindrical film that can be used for pouches or bags. Each method influences the film's properties, thickness, and application suitability, catering to diverse demands in the packaging industry.</p></p>

<p><p>The friction conveyor system market consists of various types, each tailored for specific applications. Floor type conveyors operate on the ground level, facilitating the transport of goods across flat surfaces. Overhead type systems utilize ceiling space for suspended product movement, optimizing floor space. Shuttle type conveyors employ a shuttle mechanism to transport materials between fixed points, enhancing efficiency in handling. Pallet type systems utilize pallets for transporting heavy or bulky items, providing stability and ease of handling in industrial settings.</p></p>

<p><p>The Embolic Prevention Systems market consists of various devices designed to prevent embolic events during surgical procedures or cardiovascular interventions. Distal occlusion devices block blood flow downstream to capture embolic material, while distal filters are designed to trap debris before it can enter the systemic circulation. Proximal occlusion devices, on the other hand, prevent emboli from traveling upstream and into important arteries. Together, these technologies play a critical role in enhancing patient safety during invasive procedures.</p></p>

Doppler ultrasound devices use high-frequency sound waves to detect and amplify the fetal heartbeat, providing a non-invasive and portable option for monitoring. Electronic fetal monitoring (EFM) devices involve continuous tracking of fetal heart rate and uterine contractions, offering comprehensive data for medical staff during labor. Both types enhance fetal health assessments and assist in timely medical interventions when necessary.</p></p>

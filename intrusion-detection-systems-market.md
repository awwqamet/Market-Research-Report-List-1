<p><strong>The Intrusion Detection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Network IDS/IPS Appliances</li><li>Host IDS/IPS</li><li>Network IDS/IPS Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) and Intrusion Prevention Systems (IPS) market comprises several types. Network IDS/IPS appliances are dedicated hardware devices analyzing network traffic for malicious activity. Host IDS/IPS operates on individual devices, monitoring system calls and file integrity for intrusions. Network IDS/IPS software, on the other hand, is installed on servers or endpoints, providing flexibility and scalability for monitoring and responding to threats. Together, they enhance security by identifying and mitigating potential cybersecurity threats in real-time.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Traveling Water Screen System</li><li>Static Water Screen System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Water Screen Systems market comprises two main types: Traveling Water Screen Systems and Static Water Screen Systems. Traveling Water Screens are automated devices that move across the water's surface to filter out debris and aquatic life, commonly used in power plants and water treatment facilities. In contrast, Static Water Screen Systems are fixed structures that rely on gravity to draw water through screens, effectively trapping larger particles without mechanical movement, thus offering a more energy-efficient solution for water filtration.</p></p>

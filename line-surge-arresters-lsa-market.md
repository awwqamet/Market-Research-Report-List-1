<p><ul><li>Non-Gapped Line Arrester (NGLA)</li><li>Externally Gapped Line Arrester (EGLA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Line Surge Arresters (LSAs) are devices designed to protect power lines and electrical equipment from voltage surges, particularly those caused by lightning strikes. The market features two primary types: Non-Gapped Line Arresters (NGLA), which utilize integrated components without an air gap for immediate surge suppression; and Externally Gapped Line Arresters (EGLA), which incorporate an air gap to provide enhanced protection against more severe surges. Both types are crucial for ensuring reliability and safety in electrical networks.</p></p>

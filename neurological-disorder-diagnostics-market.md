<p><p>The Neurological Disorder Diagnostics Market comprises primarily two types: imaging techniques and in vitro diagnostics techniques. Imaging techniques, such as MRI and CT scans, provide visual insights into brain structures and functions, aiding in the detection of abnormalities. In vitro diagnostics involve testing biological samples, like blood or cerebrospinal fluid, to identify biomarkers associated with neurological conditions. Together, these approaches enhance diagnostic accuracy, enabling timely and targeted treatment for various neurological disorders, thereby improving patient outcomes.</p></p>

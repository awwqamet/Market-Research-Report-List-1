<p><p>The conductive paste market is categorized into three main types: silver-based, copper-based, and aluminum-based pastes. Silver-based pastes offer excellent conductivity and are often used in high-performance applications, such as solar cells and electronics. Copper-based pastes provide a cost-effective alternative with good conductivity and are favored in various industrial applications. Aluminum-based pastes, while less conductive than silver or copper, are lighter and more affordable, making them suitable for specific sectors like automotive and packaging. Each type serves distinct market needs based on performance, cost, and application requirements.</p></p>

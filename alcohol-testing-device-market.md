<p><strong>The Alcohol Testing Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcohol testing device market is divided into two main types: contact and contactless devices. Contact devices require users to blow into a mouthpiece, allowing for direct sampling of breath alcohol levels. This method is widely used in law enforcement and personal breathalyzers. In contrast, contactless devices utilize sensors to detect alcohol presence from a distance, often using infrared technology. These devices provide quick readings without direct interaction, making them suitable for applications like workplace testing and public safety monitoring.</p></p>

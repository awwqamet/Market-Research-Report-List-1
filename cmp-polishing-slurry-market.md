<p><p>The CMP polishing slurry market comprises three key types: alumina slurry, colloidal silica slurry, and ceria slurry. Alumina slurry is known for its aggressive polishing action and is commonly used for metal surfaces. Colloidal silica slurry offers superior planarization and surface finish, making it ideal for semiconductor applications. Ceria slurry is favored for its effectiveness in polishing oxides, particularly in advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Each type caters to specific needs in the semiconductor and electronics industries, enhancing substrate quality and performance.</p></p>

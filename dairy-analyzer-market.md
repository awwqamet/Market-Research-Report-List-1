<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dairy Analyzer market consists of two primary types: ultrasonic and infrared analyzers. Ultrasonic analyzers use sound waves to measure parameters such as fat, protein, and lactose content in milk, offering high precision and quick results. Infrared analyzers, on the other hand, employ infrared light to determine similar components by measuring absorbance at specific wavelengths. Both methods enhance quality control in dairy processing, ensuring product consistency and compliance with industry standards, while catering to the needs of milk producers and processors.</p></p>

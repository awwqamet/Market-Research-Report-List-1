<p><p>Engineering contracting encompasses different market types, primarily general contracting and subcontracting. General contracting involves overseeing entire construction projects, managing budgets, timelines, and subcontractors while ensuring compliance with regulations. In contrast, subcontracting focuses on specialized tasks within a project, where subcontractors are hired by general contractors to perform specific functions, such as electrical or plumbing work. Both markets are essential for effective project management and successful delivery, enabling collaboration between various skilled teams to achieve the desired outcomes.</p></p>

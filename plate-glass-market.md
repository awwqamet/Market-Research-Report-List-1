<p><strong>The Plate Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Process</li><li>Rolling Process</li><li>Horizontal Sheet Process</li><li>Vertical Drawing Process</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plate glass market features several manufacturing processes. The **float process** involves molten glass being floated on molten tin to create smooth sheets. The **rolling process** involves glass being passed through rollers to achieve desired thickness. The **horizontal sheet process** forms glass in a flat plane, while the **vertical drawing process** pulls glass sheets vertically, offering unique thickness options. Additional methods, termed **others**, encompass various techniques like casting or pressing, catering to specific applications within the industry.</p></p>

<p><p>The automobile interior fasteners market comprises two main types: threaded and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, such as screws and bolts, provide secure connections through interlocking threads, allowing for easy assembly and disassembly. Non-threaded fasteners include clips, rivets, and snaps, which rely on friction or mechanical design to hold materials together without threading. Both types are essential for ensuring the stability and longevity of various interior components, enhancing vehicle safety, aesthetics, and user experience within the automotive sector.</p></p>

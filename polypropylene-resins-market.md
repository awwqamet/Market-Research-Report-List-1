<p><p>Polypropylene resins are categorized into several types based on their molecular structure and properties. Homopolymer polypropylene is known for its high strength and rigidity. Random copolymer polypropylene offers improved clarity and flexibility, making it suitable for packaging. Impact copolymer polypropylene combines the benefits of both homopolymer and random copolymer, providing enhanced toughness and impact resistance. Additionally, there are specialty grades and modified variants, collectively referred to as "others," which cater to specific industrial applications and performance requirements.</p></p>

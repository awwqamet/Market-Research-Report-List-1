<p><strong>The Li-ion Battery Fuse Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Low Impedance"</li><li>"High Impedance"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Li-ion battery fuse market comprises two main types based on impedance: low impedance and high impedance. Low impedance fuses provide minimal resistance, allowing for higher current flow and rapid response to short circuits, enhancing safety in high-performance applications. In contrast, high impedance fuses are designed to limit current, offering better protection against overcurrent conditions while minimizing energy loss. The choice between these types depends on application requirements, including performance, safety levels, and thermal management considerations.</p></p>

<p><p>The dental turbine handpiece market is divided into two main types: inside exhaust and outside exhaust handpieces. Inside exhaust handpieces expel air and debris internally, minimizing environmental exposure and assisting in achieving a clear working area. In contrast, outside exhaust handpieces vent air and debris externally, providing better visibility for the dentist but potentially dispersing particles into the operatory. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to different preferences and procedural requirements in dental practices.</p></p>

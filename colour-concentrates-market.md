<p><ul><li>Solid Colour Concentrates</li><li>Liquid Colour Concentrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colour concentrates market comprises two primary types: solid and liquid colour concentrates. Solid colour concentrates, often in the form of powder or pellets, are used in processes like injection moulding and extrusion, offering high pigment loading and ease of storage. Liquid colour concentrates, however, are typically used in applications needing uniform dispersion, such as coatings and plastics. These provide better mixing capabilities and are ideal for applications requiring precise colour matching and a reduced risk of contamination.</p></p>

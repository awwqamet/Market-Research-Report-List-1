<p><strong>The Cement Additive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber</li><li>Chemical</li><li>Mineral</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cement additive market comprises three main types: fiber, chemical, and mineral additives. Fiber additives enhance the tensile strength and toughness of cement, making it more durable and resistant to cracking. Chemical additives, such as superplasticizers and retarders, improve workability, reduce water demand, and influence setting times. Mineral additives, including silica fume and fly ash, enhance the performance of cement by improving its durability and resistance to chemical attacks while reducing environmental impact through the utilization of waste materials.</p></p>

<p><p>CNC surface grinders are categorized mainly into horizontal and vertical types, each serving distinct applications. Horizontal surface grinders utilize a horizontal spindle orientation, ideal for producing flat surfaces on larger workpieces. They allow for efficient material removal and are commonly used in manufacturing settings. In contrast, vertical surface grinders feature a vertical spindle, which is better suited for smaller parts and intricate profiles. This design ensures precision in grinding tasks, catering to diverse industry requirements, including automotive and aerospace sectors.</p></p>
